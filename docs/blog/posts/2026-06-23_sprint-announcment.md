# Announcing the Geo4Lib Community Sprint Summer 2026

From **August 17-21, 2026** we will be holding a week-long community sprint. This is a chance to come together to work on shared projects, contribute to community documentation, or fix software bugs. It can also be a week to set aside time to work on a new or side project, while having a group on hand to discuss and answer questions.

<!-- more -->

The sprint will start with a kickoff meeting where we will review our goals and volunteer to contribute towards various issues. This will be followed by short standup meetings every day to check-in and plan our next activities. Participants do not need to attend every meeting, and can just show up when they are available or just follow our activities on Slack.

## Event Dates

* Monday–Friday, August 17–21, 2026
* Daily 30 minute standup meetings: 1:00 p.m. Central Time

## How to Join

Add the event from the [Geo4Lib Community Calendar](/../../events). Zoom links will be shared on Slack and added to the calendar event closer to the kickoff.

## Proposed Work Tracks

Participants may bring their own projects or contribute to ongoing work. The proposed tracks for the 2026 sprint include:

### OpenGeoMetadata

* Repository documentation and cleanup
* GitHub Actions for validation, deduplication, and quality assessments

### GeoBlacklight

* Plugin cleanup
* Version 6 development work

### Community Governance and Updates

* Review the Geo4LibCamp shared notes and identify projects or discussion topics for upcoming meetings
* Suggest edits, report broken links, and flag outdated information on program websites

### Data Processing

* Update scripts and documentation for creating COGs and PMTiles
* Georeference a collection of maps using Allmaps
* Collect and archive a collection of public geospatial datasets
